Instructions (Line By Line):

Replace blue numbers for values below. Output data will appear in the "Valuation Model Outputs" section.

Revenue - Revenue for Year 0 (millions).
% Growth - Predict revenue growth percentage for the next 10 years.
% Gross Profit Margin - Predict gross margin percentage for the next 10 years.
Operating Expenses - Predict dollar amount (millions) of operating expenses for the next 10 years.
Interest Expense/Other Expenses - Predict interest/other expenses (millions) to arrive at net income for the next 10 years.
Net Income - Net income for Year 0. Net incomes for Year 1 through Year 10 are calculated.
Book Value - (Assets - Liabilities) at the end of Year 0 (millions).
Number of Share Outstanding - Average shares outstanding for Year 0 (millions). Optional as it does not affect the predicted stock price.
% Change - Predict stock buybacks or dilution for the next 10 years. Optional as it does not affect the predicted stock price.
Current Stock Price - Company's current stock price. Optional as it does not affect the predicted stock price.
Competitor PE Ratio - A competitor's PE ratio. Optional as it does not affect the predicted stock price.
Marginal Tax Rate - Company's marginal tax rate. 15% is is the default value because it is the average corporate tax rate.
Equity Capital Opportunity Cost - Desired rate of return on investment. 10% is the default value because it is the average index stock market return.
Terminal Growth Rate - Terminal growth rate of business at Year 10. 5% is the default.
